A lamington is an Australian cake made from squares of
butter cake or sponge cake coated in an outer layer of
chocolate sauce and rolled in desiccated coconut. The
thin mixture is absorbed into the outside of the sponge cake
and left to set, giving the cake a distinctive texture.
