About Us

Hello!I'm Sharon.I'm an artisan baker who has had a lifetime interest in traditional baking methods. I received my education in Paris. Currently, I own a bakery named Sun And Moon Cafe in the Center Point Plaza that specializes on traditional Singaporean baked delicacies

Traditional

At Sun And Moon Cafe, we priortize in creating our goods as faithful to the traditional taste as possible. We only rely on the traditional methods in order to preserve the wonder of Singaporean baked products.

Freshness Guranteed

Freshness is what sets us apart from others. From ingredients to the actual baking process, utmost care is taken to ensure that every item is fresh and healthy.

Quality You Can Trust

We pride ourselves in the quality of our goods and the dedication and effort we put into each and every one of our products. Our current business parthners include James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra.
