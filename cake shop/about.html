About Us

Hello! I'm Sharon. I'm an artisan-baker with lifelong passion in traditional baking techniques. I was trained in Paris. Currently I operate a bakery called Tropical Treats that specialize in traditional Australian baked goods, near the Willows Shopping Center.

Traditional

At Tropical Treats, we priortize in creating our goods as faithful to the traditional taste as possible. We only rely on the traditional methods in order to preserve the wonder of Australian baked products.

Freshness Guranteed

Freshness is what sets us apart from others. From ingredients to the actual baking process, utmost care is taken to ensure that every item is fresh and healthy.

Quality You Can Trust

We pride ourselves in the quality of our goods and the dedication and effort we put into each and every one of our products. Our current business parthners include James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra.
